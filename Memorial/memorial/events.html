National Reading Day :

What is the National Reading Day ?

National Reading Day is an annual event that falls on the death
anniversary of PN Panicker, the father of 'Library Movement in
Kerala' in order to encourage reading among students all
around the country. Due to novel coronavirus, PN Panicker
Foundation has organized activities and carried initiatives that
motivate reading online.

Which is the day of Reading a Book ?

Every year it is celebrated on June 19, which is the death anniversary of PN Panicker.
